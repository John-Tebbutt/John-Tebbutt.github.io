ACCOMMODATION

The Cherry Lodge apartments have been fashioned from a former farmhouse and stables which date back to the late 1800's. They are set in 1.3 acres of tranquil grounds which also feature their own private swimming pool.

Cherry Lodge provides a charming retreat for a relaxing break and an excellent base from which to explore this captivating area of France.

Each of the apartments has been sympathetically renovated to retain the original features, such as the characteristic oak beams and shuttered windows.

There are four apartments to choose from at Cherry Lodge: Les Lauriers, La Lavande, Les Tournesols et Les Vignes (click photos to enlarge/shrink).

Les Lauriers is a 1 bedroom ground floor apartment sleeping 1-4 with its own private garden area and is also part of the old stable block.

La Lavande is also a 1 bedroom ground floor apartment sleeping 1-4 and is also part of the old stable block. Access is via the courtyard at the front of the complex with the feature stone well.

Les Tournesols is a 2 bedroom apartment sleeping 1-5 situated on the top floor of the old stable block and is accessed via a block of steps at the back of the complex.

Les Vignes is a 2 bedroom apartment sleeping 1-5 and is situated above the main farm house, having its own roof terrace with BBQ overlooking the swimming pool. Access is via a separate staircase at the rear of the complex.

All the apartments have their own bathroom, fully equipped kitchen with fridge, television and DVD. A large chest freezer is also available for frozen foods.

Bed linen, pillows and towels are provided on arrival. There is a laundry with washing machines and a tumble dryer if required. We can supply a cot and highchair if needed.

There is a secure children's play area and plenty of toys and books for the younger holidaymakers. There is also a well stocked DVD library and Wi-Fi broadband internet access is also available.
